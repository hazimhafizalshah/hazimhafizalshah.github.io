Projects

  NerveDrive is an attempt at innovation by simultaneously utilizing both Brain-Computer Interfaces and Virtual Reality in a driving simulator that emulates the driver's license tests.

In order to achieve a sastifactory level of visual realism, I created the 3D models of the car, props and enviroment involved in this game.

By showcasing this game, I was awarded in the Graduate Digital, Invention, Innovation & Development 2024: Gold Medal, Industrial Award, Best Presenter and Best Poster.

  Leftenan Adnan is an online realtime multiplayer FPS. Players can swap between two characters, wielding varied weaponry such as rifles, bazookas and grenades. The first player to achieve 5 kills wins the game.

This game was created using Unity Engine with Photon as the networking framework. The movement and actions of the characters are synced between all players, with behaviours such as projectiles and physics implemented.

  Fantasy Frontier is a local multiplayer turn-based game that be played on both Windows and Android. Players take turns to either move on the board, conquer tiles or battle others. Fantasy Frontier features a wildcard system, that brings almost twenty unique effects to make every match a fresh experience.

  Angry Birds 3D is a fan-game; one that exhibits mechanics such as physics-based gameplayer, destructible environments and cute characters.



In creation of this game, I took the chance to learn and make stylized 3D models of the game characters, interactable props and the environment. Every model was texture painted to achieve the stylized aesthetic.

  Lego PPAP is a lighthearted comedic piece showing an animation of Lego minifigure dancing Pen Pineapple Apple Pen that went viral years ago. To achieve this, I modeled, rigged and animated while referencing the source video, studying its choreography.

  Reben is a concept online employment website dedicated to people with special needs. People with special needs should be able to find jobs and be employed as easily as others who do not face the same challenges.

In order to achieve this aim, I designed a website that puts accessibility at the forefront. With features such as customization, color-blind friendly design, high contrast theming Reben shows the potential of a direly needed resource for the specially abled.
